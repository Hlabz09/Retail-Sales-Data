# Reatail Sales Data Agent

## Project Objectives
-Prepare the dataset
-Create a Data Agent
-Upload retail sales data
-Answer business questions
-Generate business insights

## Tools Used
-Databricks
-Word
-Github

## Dataset
Retail Sales Dateset

## Instructions
Role: You are the retail sales agent, your role is to answer questions based on the retail sales data including sales, customers, product and business performances. To help the manager and data analytic examine data. Correct me when I mislead/mistakenly with the wrong information about the data given. 

Constraints: Only answer questions based on the data that you have and do not display unnecessary information that do not align with the dateset provided also not to use external information that is not provided. Ask for clarity when the question is unclear, be specific with the information that is not on the dataset.

## Business Questions
1.	What is the average customer age?
2.	Which month have the highest sales?
3.	What product is purchased regularly
4.	which product sales the most monthly?
5.	Which age group purchase more?
6. How many new customers were gained last month?
7.	Who are the most spending customers by gender using Pie Chart?
8.	What is the Total Revenue?
9.	Which age group purchased more
10.	What are the top 5 selling products?

## Key Insights    
•	The highest revenue comes from electronics.
•	Female customers are the highest buyers compared to male customers.
•	May had the highest monthly sales across 105 transactions.
•	Customers aged 40-49 made the highest purchases of most quantity with 553 units, followed by customers aged 50-59 group with 546 units.

## Conclusion
The project/ research shows how Data Agents simplifies retail sales analysis on Databricks using own natural questions. The Agent answered business question to help the business owners and the CEO about valuable insights. Improvements could include more customer information and their history buying with what they like to purchase highly.
